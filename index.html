Get Jacked

Summary

Get Jacked is my love child! I like lifting and I want to have a personal trainer with me at all times. Get Jacked is that, a personal trainer and a gym tracking app rolled into one thing. It's a website that behaves like a phone app on your iPhone or android device. Get Jacked keeps your gym progress on track. It is based on exercise science principles which are a hobby of mine. Training using get jacked follows scientific best practices and will get you jacked if you can consistently adhere to the recommendations.
